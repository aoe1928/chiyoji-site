🔔 Announcement: the *Maboroshi no Märchen / Shadow of the Memoir* cassette and our Golden Week campaign

To coincide with the music video premiere and digital release on April 25, 2025, we will release a cassette edition of the EP *Maboroshi no Märchen / Shadow of the Memoir*.

We are also planning a merchandise sale available only during Golden Week. A limited number of special-value sets will be offered, so please take a look.

As another new project, we will release Second Waltz’s first ZINE! It is a compact booklet, but one filled with our thoughts and ideas.

We will share more information as it becomes available. Stay tuned!
