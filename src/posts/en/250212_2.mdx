Our new EP will be released on February 14!

Following the attention received by the previous release *Telepath Girl*, Chiyoji’s home-recording project Second Waltz will release the long-awaited EP *Ma mémoire* on Friday, February 14.

The five lyrical miniatures on this EP were composed while Second Waltz leader Chiyoji traveled throughout Japan. It compiles the feelings and impressions gathered in those places, under the theme of a “memoir.”

Life is beautiful because it is filled with memories that can never be repeated. Please listen to this slightly bittersweet work—one that we hope will leave you feeling uplifted by the end.

Second Waltz will also hold a release show at Kissa Senko in Nishi-Ogikubo on Saturday, February 15. CDs featuring these songs and other limited merchandise will be available, so please check them out as well.
