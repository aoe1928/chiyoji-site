Purgatorio has released a new music video for “Bunjochi no Märchen”!

This ambitious video captures the vast landscapes of a residential subdivision in Hokota, Ibaraki, using drones and other techniques. We were also joined by Yusuke Yoshikawa ([X account](https://x.com/yuwave2009)), well known for documenting Japan’s remote and marginal housing developments.

Chiyoji plays bass on the version included on the EP *The Mavic*. For the music video, I replaced that part with my own bass performance, recorded new vocals, and improved the mix.

Please take a look!
