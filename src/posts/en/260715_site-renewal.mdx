Chiyoji's website has received a major update!

Until now, this site has mainly featured music and live-show announcements. In the future, I also plan to write about life hacks, technology, and other topics. To prepare for that, I have made it much easier to find past posts and browse the subjects you are interested in.

### Search and Tag Filters

A search box and tag filters are now available beside the blog on desktop. On smartphones, they can be opened with the “Search & filter” button.

You can search article titles and text, or narrow the list using hierarchical tags such as:

- Music › Second Waltz › Live
- Music › Second Waltz › Merchandise
- Music › Purgatorio › Live
- Site News

More categories, including Life Hacks and Technology, will be added as new articles are published.

### English Version

The site menus and articles are now available in English. You can switch languages using the “日本語 / English” links at the bottom of each page.

English versions of previous posts have also been added. I hope this helps more people around the world discover the music and activities of Second Waltz and Purgatorio.

### A Better Article List

Blog navigation has been improved so that the current, previous, and next pages are easier to understand. Text, images, spacing, and menus have also been adjusted for better reading on both computers and smartphones.

Images inside articles now use a more comfortable size, making live-show flyers and other visuals easier to view without taking over the entire screen.

### Display and Security Improvements

An issue that briefly made text difficult to read when opening a page has been fixed. The software used to build the site has also been updated to improve security and stability.

I will continue posting music news along with a wider variety of articles. Thank you for visiting!
